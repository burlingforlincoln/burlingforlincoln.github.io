Primary Election April 6, 2021 - Omaha and Lincoln

MENU Eric Burling Lincoln City Council - At Large Welcome! Thank you for your willingness to serve by running for public office. And, thank you especially for being willing to let voters know what you think and why on many issues of concern to voters.

At any time while you’re answering, the autosave feature saves your work every minute or so. If you want more time, you may click on "Save & Continue" which allows you to come back later to complete the questionnaire. Once you click “Save & Submit for Review,” your answers are complete. You may also print your responses for your records.

Our purpose is to display your answers just as you have entered them. Please tell us why we should vote for you, not why we shouldn't vote for your opponent. If we have any questions, we will contact you.

Please contact us at kathy@voterinformation.org if you have difficulties or prefer to answer by email or mail.

  1. [CITY REGULATIONS - Lincoln] The role of city government, regulating personal activity, should include: (Note A, B, C, D and/or E and add comments to clarify, if you wish.) —A.
